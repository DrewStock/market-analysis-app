Welcome to the Bus Mall Product Picker focus group!

To begin, please click on the displayed image that most closely represents a product that you would be interested in purchasing. Each time that you do this, your selection will be counted as a vote for that product. After you've voted 15 times, a chart will display your voting results. Then, if you wish to continue voting, please press Continue. To view all voting results, please press All Results, then click Reset to continue voting. Thank you for your participation!
